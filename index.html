Our Mission

Rural wants to provide every child on this earth with an opportunity to gain knowledge about the world, the power to act in it and the desire to be self-reliant and compassionate.

Projects

We have partnered with Ghana Makes A Difference(GMAD) that provides housing and necessities for children who have been rescued from human trafficking, abuse and neglect. The organization constructed a new school to serve their children and others in the community. They employed full-time teachers, a special needs teacher and a vocational trainer. Students can spend one year in the program and enter and exit the program at times.
